Awards

This page holds a list of the awards that I have received up to the present day that range from volunteering and scholarships to hobbies and preivious extracurriculars.

Note: These are outside of competing in coding-based events where ethically was possible. For coding competitions, the results and more information can be found on the Coding & Game Design Projects page and the Event Planning Projects page.

Awards From College (2022-2025)

University of Washington Tacoma Husky Volunteers 2025 Leadership Award Medal Recipient

Received for having over 300 hours of volunteering over 4 years or 200 hours of volunteering if a transfer student at the University of Washington Tacoma. I had a total of 330 hours of volunteering over 3 school years.

University of Washington Tacoma Husky Volunteers 2024 Silver Medallion Recipient

Received for having over 100 hours of volunteering during the duration of time at the University of Washington Tacoma. I had a total of 125 hours of volunteering over 2 school years.

2024-2025 Council for Campus Engagement Committee Member

Received recognition and an award from the University of Washington Tacoma for serving as a Council for Campus Engagement Committee Member during the 2024-2025 academic year.

Awards From High School (2018-2022)

2022 C.J. Knaggs Actvity Award from Bethel High School

  • This award was given to students based on their extracurricular involvement based on one's efforts by being deeply involved and helping to create a positive impact with the school and community throughout your time at Bethel High School.

2022 John Philip Sousa Award

  • For Bethel High School’s Class of 2022, I was one of two students who received this award, and it is based on leadership and musicianship which is entered into a national database.

Western International Band Clinic (WIBC)

  • Auditioned and was accepted in the 2018, 2019, 2020, and 2021 honor band programs for clarinet performance and attended the 2020 and 2021 honor bands.

2022 Pacific Lutheran University Northwest High School Honor Band Member

  • Was accepted after being nominated for clarinet performance into the 2022 Pacific Lutheran University Northwest High School Honor Band (PLUNWHSHB) and attended the honor band.

Mountain Regional (Washington) Music Educators Association (MRMEA) Solo and Ensemble Festival

  • Earned a rating of Excellent (II (for the numeric score)) for a solo clarinet performance at the 2019 Mountain Regional (Washington) Music Educators Association (MRMEA) Solo and Ensemble Festival.
  • an Excellent (II+ (for the numeric score)) rating for a clarinet ensemble performance at the 2019 Mountain Regional (Washington) Music Educators Association (MRMEA) Solo and Ensemble Festival.
  • Earned a rating of Excellent (II+ (for a numeric score)) for a solo clarient performance at the 2020 Mountain Regional (Washington) Music Educators Association (MRMEA) Solo and Ensemble Festival.
  • Earned a Good (III+/III (for a numeric score)) rating for a woodwind ensemble performance at the 2020 Mountain Regional (Washington) Music Educators Association (MRMEA) Solo and Ensemble Festival.
  • Earned an Excellent (II) for a solo clarinet performance at the 2022 Mountain Regional (Washington) Music Educators Association (MRMEA) Solo and Ensemble Festival.
  • Earned a Superior (I) for a woodwind duet performance at the 2022 Mountain Regional (Washington) Music Educators Association (MRMEA) Solo and Ensemble Festival.

Washington Music Educators Association (WMEA) All-State Honor Band program

  • Was accepted after auditioning for clarinet performance into the Washington Music Educators Association (WMEA) 2021 All-State Honor Band program of the 2021 All-Northwest Honor Band Program and attended.

Mountain Regional (Washington) Music Educators Association (MRMEA) Large Ensemble Festival

  • Earned a rating of Excellent/Superior (II/I (for a numeric score)) as a part of a large ensemble performance of the 2018-2019 Bethel High School Concert Band's performance at the 2019 Mountain Regional (Washington) Music Educators Association (MRMEA) Large Ensemble Festival.
